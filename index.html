About Me

About Me? hmmm where should I start? I was born and raised in New York City, Harlem to be exact. I grew in the 90's and 2000's, which was a wild time to be where I'm from. I Left in 2004 and joined the Military, where I traveled the world serving the country and all that other fun stuff. Met my wife, left the military, had my son and packed up and moved to California to go to school. I got my Bachelors in Game Art and Design down in Los Angeles, then moved to the Bay Area in 2014.

In the last few years I did alot of freelance work while doing senior management at logistics companies (military background), until I decided to start my own company, an immersive entertainment studio focusing on media content designed for emerging technologies like virtual reality. If im not churning away at 3d art assets, designing interactive content or attempting to learn C#, I'm playing Minecraft, Brawlhalla, or Monster Hunter World with my 9 year old son.
