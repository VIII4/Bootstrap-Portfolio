About Me

hmmm where should I start? Born and raised in New York City. I Left in 2004 and joined the Military, where I traveled the world serving the country and all that other fun stuff. After Honorable discharging from the US Navy, my wife, newborn son and I packed up and moved to California to go to school. I got my Bachelors in Game Art and Design down in Los Angeles, then moved to the Bay Area in 2014.

After graduating, I did alot of 3D design freelance work while doing senior management at logistics companies (military background), until I decided to start my own company, an immersive entertainment studio focusing on web content designed for emerging technologies like virtual reality. If im not churning away at 3d art assets, designing interactive content or attempting to learn C#, I'm playing Minecraft, Brawlhalla, or Monster Hunter World with my 9 year old son.
